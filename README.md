# SmartDog

## Mechanical Design of a Simple Quadruped Robot

### Project Overview

SmartDog is a simple four-legged robot designed using Tinkercad. The project focuses on creating a basic mechanical design that can stand and walk while applying simple mechanical engineering concepts.

---

## Features

- Four-legged robot
- High-strength plastic body (ABS/PLA)
- 8 Degrees of Freedom (8 DOF)
- Proposed Servo Motors
- Stable body design

---

## Mechanical Design

The robot has a rectangular body with four legs. Each leg includes a hip joint, a knee joint, and a foot. The design is simple and suitable for a beginner mechanical robot project.

---

## Motor Selection

Servo motors are proposed because they provide accurate angle control and are suitable for moving the robot's leg joints.

---

## Stability

The center of gravity is placed near the middle of the robot body to improve balance during movement.

---

## Walking Method

The robot uses a Crawl Gait, where one leg moves at a time while the other three legs remain on the ground for better stability.
